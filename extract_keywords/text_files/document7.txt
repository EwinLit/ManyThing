The Evolution of Social Media
Social media has evolved from simple platforms for connecting friends to complex ecosystems that shape global communication. Today, platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram are integral parts of personal and professional life. Social media has redefined marketing, news distribution, and even political movements. However, with its rise, issues such as misinformation, online harassment, and privacy concerns have emerged. As society continues to grapple with these challenges, the future of social media remains uncertain.
